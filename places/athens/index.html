Athens

visited studied

The cradle of democracy and Western philosophy — marble temples, olive light, and two and a half thousand years of ideas still echoing through the city’s streets.

Why this place

Athens sits at the intersection of everywhere I’ve been and everything I’ve studied. To walk up to the Acropolis is to walk into the origin story of so much of how the West thinks about politics, art, and reason.

What I studied

  • The birth of Athenian democracy and the institutions of the polis.
  • Classical philosophy — Socrates, Plato, and Aristotle.
  • The architecture of the Acropolis and the Doric order.

Highlights

  • The Parthenon at golden hour.
  • The Ancient Agora — the literal marketplace of ideas.
  • The view over the city from Lycabettus Hill.
